NEO
January 2018 - Present
NEO aims to design an online crowdsourcing game with a purpose, which invites people all over the world to team up and map the complex neural network. We are working with our clients at Seung Lab, Princeton University to create a gaming world where players discover, investigate, collaborate and challenge each other to expand a land of neurons.

The goal is to incorporate the large dataset from Seung Lab and create an engaging, purposeful game that brings more people into this meaningful activity. NEO uses an advanced AI technology that can detect detailed neuron models from micro captures of a brain. Working with the AI, players discover the structure of neurons.
