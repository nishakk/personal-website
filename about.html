about me
I'm a designer and a coder. I enjoy working on complex problems and turning them into simple and intuitive interfaces. I have a background in computer science and engineering which helps me in understanding the feasibility of solutions while my designing skills help me to think out of the box.
As a student and as a software engineer, I often find myself exploring ways to improve user experience by placing myself in the shoes of the users. From stressing on creating perfect PowerPoint presentations to aligning elements on a web page for accessibility, I have always taken special interest to cater to the needs of the target audience. I am currently focusing on honing my UX skills to be able to understand and empathize with users to create meaningful interactions between devices and people.
I'm drawn towards anything artistic. I love sketching, acryclic painting, photography and reading. Being a UX engineer, I want to interweave my passion for art and technology.
